3 TWO TANKS EXAMPLE
4 ==================
5 
6 The discharge of air from one tank into another through a nozzle. The
7 problem only makes sense, if you think of the flow through the nozzle as a
8 much more complex problem which you have to solve with - let's say 3D CFD. So
9 the two tanks are the macroscopic and the nozzle is the microscopic problem.
10 This is also a backward mapping problem if you assume that the range of
11 inputs is unknown a-priori. twoTanksMacroscopicProblem uses the MoDeNa
12 interface library to embed an even simpler model for the flow rate. While
13 twoTanksFullProblem implements it fully integrated.
